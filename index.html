About Us

We have been creating delightful moments in our customers' daily lives for the past 21 years. Our world class chocolates are made using the best ingredients, in abundance. We are proud about our heritage, the quality & passion of our workmanship, and you - our customers. Above all, we created The Ooty Chocolates with one single vision - to be your everyday partner in creating delightful moments.

After all, life is to be celebrated everyday. So, don't wait for a special occasion to tell your loved ones how much you love them. Don't wait for a special achievement to appreciate the efforts of your dear ones. Celebrate them!

Our carefully selected packaging ranges from simple cellophane bags, hand tied with pretty ribbons to our unique hand-crafted fabric covered boxes. Our traditional Continental ballotins are available in classic gold or silver, contemporary single colours and pretty floral designs.

This website is a new initiative from our side to make it easier for you to enjoy your daily sweet moments. The truly delightful moments!

Happy Everyday!
